Our story

A relaxed local favourite with a modern coastal feel.

Whether you're stopping for a drink after a seafront walk, booking bottomless brunch with friends, planning a Sunday dinner, or arranging a private celebration, The New Sundial is an easy place to gather, eat and enjoy the coast.

Situated on Sea Road, just minutes from South Marine Park and Ocean Beach Pleasure Park — with space for over 200 guests inside and 150 on the sun-trap patio.

Food & drinks

Pub classics, brunch favourites and fresh plates for everyone.

Clear menu sections help guests quickly choose what they came for.

Private events

From brunches to celebrations of life, make it feel personal.

The venue offers large open-plan seating, a spacious front patio and private event spaces suitable for birthdays, weddings, corporate events and family gatherings.
